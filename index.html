About Julie

A legacy of artistry, passion and uncompromising quality

15+ Years of Floral Mastery

Julie is a fully established, highly respected florist with over 15 years of experience creating exquisite arrangements for the most important moments in life. Her reputation is built on uncompromising quality, personal service, and an extraordinary ability to translate your vision into floral art.

Specialising in dignified, deeply personal funeral tributes, elegant luxury bouquets, and completely bespoke designs. Julie is renowned for creating pieces other florists decline — if you can imagine it, she will craft it with precision and beauty.

Every stem is hand-selected from premium growers; every arrangement is finished with meticulous attention to detail. Hundreds of clients return year after year, leaving glowing 5-star reviews for service that goes above and beyond.
